Semi‑Blind Source Separation for Unmanned Aerial Vehicle audition

Jin Xuan Teh*, Norihiro Takamune†, Hiroshi Saruwatari†, Benjamin Yen*, Michael Kingan* & Yusuke Hioka*

* Acoustics & Vibration Research Centre, University of Auckland, Auckland, NZ

† The University of Tokyo, Tokyo, Japan

DOI: 10.1016/j.apacoust.2025.111019

Metrics

  • SDRi: source‑to‑distortion ratio improvement
  • STOIi: short‑time objective intelligibility improvement

Paper summary

The paper presents a semi-blind source separation framework for unmanned aerial vehicle audition based on supervised independent low-rank matrix analysis. Variants incorporating recursive covariance estimation (RCSCME) and weighted filtering (WF) achieve notable improvements in SDRi and STOIi at both 3 m and 5 m measurement distances. Refer to the publication for full methodological and experimental details.
